What´s happening?

You start in a world that is always between dark and fire and relies on the first flame.
You get awakened by a ringing bell which tells you that the first flame is fading.

The fading of the flame is recognizable by an eclipse that is permanently happening.
(as seen in the background)

You´re also called "The Ashen One" and "Unkindled" because you tried to link the flame but failed.
Now you got awakened again in order to try and link the flame again.

The lords which are Ashen Ones who linked the flame already are leaving their thrones and your job is
to bring them back and link the flame.

These Lords are Aldrich,Ludleth,Yhorm,Lorian and the Abyss Watchers who you need to face as bosses in order
to bring them back to their thrones.

Only, in truth... The Lords will abandon their thrones.
and the Unkindled will rise.
