Rating, awards and metacritic

Dark Souls 3 won many awards such as the Golden Joystick and also the steam award for a huge hate-love relationship.
It also won the GOTY(2016)Award from IGN and the Gamescom-Award for the best action RPG.

One of the most famous critic-sites "metacritics" gave the game a metascore of 89 which is pretty impressive if you ask me.

Personal opinion:

My personal opinion on the game is pretty close to the metacritic score. The game is an adventure that needs to be experienced if you wanna call yourself a gamer.
There are only some small bugs in the game and the mechanics feel insanely smooth.

But most importantly the music... The music is crazy... crazyly good. It fits every moment. Fighting or exploring, a track
that fits the emotions is always by your side.
