Heard about Dark Souls yet?

Dark Souls is a RPG (Roll-Play-Game) that is on the market since 2011.
It´s one of many games which count to the "Souls"-series. It contains three parts which are all based on one huge lore.

    Timeline of releases

  • 2009 Demon's Souls
  • 2011 Dark Souls
  • 2014 Dark Souls II
  • 2015 Dark Souls II: Scholar of the First Sin
  • 2016 Dark Souls III
  • 2018 Dark Souls: Remastered

The atmosphere which is created troughout the game is nothing you could describe in words. Just incredible.

The series itself is seen in a huge hate-love relationship because it actually requires the ability to adapt and gain perfection.
Trust me, many controllers didn´t survive the whole play-trough.

One slight mistake can end up in the "You Died"-screen which is highly famous for the "Souls"-series and has never changend since the first part.

You wont find guides in here but tips that helped me get trough the game the first time.
I´ll feature my experience and my own opinion on the game so don´t exspect a wiki page.

Interested? Dig some deeper in the pages and experience the joy of my favourite game-series.
